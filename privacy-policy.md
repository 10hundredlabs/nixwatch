NixWatch Privacy Policy

NixWatch does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

NixWatch is a Wear OS watch face. It may display information provided locally by Wear OS complication providers selected by the user, such as battery level, step count, weather, calendar, or sunrise/sunset information. This information is displayed on the watch face only and is not sent by NixWatch to any server.

NixWatch does not require an account, does not use analytics, does not include advertising, and does not contain in-app purchases.

If you have questions, contact: 10hundredlabs@gmail.com
